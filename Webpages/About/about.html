About us

The company name is BGames. It was created by four brothers who always had an interest
in board games that their father used to collect since young. They would always play board games
together whenever they were bored. Their interest for board games grew, so much that they wanted
others to be able to experience the joy of board games, thus opening a small store located in Yio Chu Kang in 1996.
Their popularity rose across the years, expanding their business across Singapore, totalling up to 10 stores countrywide.

Company Goal

We want to spread joy all over Singapore via letting them play with boardgames and card games. We also want to intoduce Singapore
to various different types of board and card games, some of which are abit more niche than others.

What we sell

We sell a variety of board games and card games. From well known games such as Uno and Taboo, to games that only diehard fans
will ever know of like machi koro, all these games are made so that we can give new players a grand experience
for experiencing their first boardgame, and veterans the opportunity to play and experience what they head
when they touched such games for the first time.
